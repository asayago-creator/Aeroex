Engineering Clearer Air:
Aeroex Teams Up with Hacksmith Industries

Hacksmith Industries has earned a global reputation for turning the iconic gadgets of comics, movies and video games into real-world engineering marvels. But as production grew to include several CNC machines running up to 16 hours a day, atomized coolant mist created excessive humidity and poor air quality throughout the shop.

To protect their team and reduce the strain on their HVAC systems, Hacksmith partnered with Aeroex Technologies to implement a targeted source-capture solution. Recently, 20 Aeroex Mist-Fit MF-550 mist collectors were installed on Hacksmith's CNC machines, while a BlowBox BB-400 provides a dedicated blow-off station for cleaning parts safely and efficiently.

At Aeroex, we're laser-focused on creating cleaner, healthier and safer manufacturing environments. The results at Hacksmith speak for themselves: improved air quality, better working conditions and a workspace built to support innovation.
