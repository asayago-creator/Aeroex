Engineering Clearer Air:
How Aeroex Partnered with Hacksmith Industries

Hacksmith Industries is globally recognized for pushing the boundaries of mechanical engineering. But as CNC production scaled up to 25 machines running 16 hours a day, the sheer volume of atomized coolant created severe humidity and hazardous air quality on the shop floor.

To protect their team and reduce the immense strain on their HVAC systems, Hacksmith partnered with Aeroex Technologies for a targeted, duct-free filtration strategy.
